A little about me

I'm Punnee. A hair Stylist, a mom, a wife, and a lover of creating.
My business is located inside Apollo Nail and Spa in Poulsbo, Washington.
I've been doing hair for over a decade now. At first it started as a way to support my kids. But, then it grew into a passion I couldn't live without!
I love everything about self care and I encourage my clients to think about inner beauty as well as outer beauty.
